About Us

Paperstart Games staff

Paperstart Games is a project created by Rodrigo Long Chen Kashiwakura, who aims to create games for everyone and everywhere. By everyone, it means for families/friends, teenagers/adults, experts/beginners, in other words, everyone who wants to play a good game. By everywhere, it means for every game developed to be playable in any platform.

At this moment, he is running after the dream of becoming a game developer and learning new techniques in the process. He is 26 years old, studied telecommunications engineering and always loved video games.
