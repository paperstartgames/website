--- layout: page title: Objectives permalink: /objectives/ ---

Create with Passion

Create every game with such passion and determination, so that every player can feel how much work was put into every little detail

Pixel Art

Find inspiration in games from the past and bring it into the present, so that the new generations can feel what we felt years ago

Learn and Create

Learn, imagine and create. Always learning and trying to find new ways of putting everything into practice.

Feel the Music

Listen the music of the past, the present and the future. Feel it and pass it to the player

All Platforms

Port every game into every platform.

Grow the Community

Spread the love for Video Games everywhere and make the community grow. Also, make new friends in the process
